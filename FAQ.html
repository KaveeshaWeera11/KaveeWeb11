Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. Items must be in their original condition with all packaging and accessories included. For defective products, we provide free return shipping and immediate replacement.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! All orders over LKR 10,000 qualify for free express shipping. For orders below this amount, we offer flat-rate shipping at just LKR 500. All deliveries include full tracking and insurance coverage.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with your tracking number and a direct link to monitor your package's journey. Our system provides real-time updates from warehouse to your doorstep.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and cash on delivery. All transactions are securely processed with 256-bit SSL encryption for your protection.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship to all regions in Sri Lanka with plans to expand internationally in 2025. For special international requests, please contact our customer service team who can arrange custom shipping solutions.
